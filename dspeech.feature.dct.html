dct

dspeechfeature

Computes type-II DCT on 1-d signal:

y[k] = 2 f \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} x[n] \cos \pi k \frac{2n + 1}{2 N} ,

where the scaling factor f = sqrt(1 / 4 N) if k = 0, f = sqrt(1 / 2 N) otherwise,
